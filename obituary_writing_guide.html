Basic Structure

1. Announcement

Start with: "[Full name] [age] of [city], passed away [date] at [location]."

Example:

"John Robert Smith, 76, of Portland, passed away peacefully on January 15, 2024, at Central Hospital."

2. Biographical Information
  • Date and place of birth
  • Parents' names
  • Educational background
  • Career highlights
  • Military service (if applicable)
3. Family Members

List both surviving family and those who preceded in death:

  • Spouse(s)
  • Children
  • Grandchildren
  • Siblings
  • Parents
4. Personal Details
  • Hobbies and interests
  • Community involvement
  • Special achievements
  • Character traits
5. Service Information

Include:

  • Date, time, and location of services
  • Visitation details
  • Burial information
  • Memorial contribution preferences

Do's and Don'ts

Do's
  • Verify all facts and dates
  • Include full names
  • Proofread carefully
  • Consider the tone
  • Include special achievements
Don'ts
  • Include cause of death (unless desired)
  • Forget important family members
  • Include sensitive information
  • Rush the writing process
  • Forget to include service details

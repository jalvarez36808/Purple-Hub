Contacting Immediate Family

Priority Contact List

  • ✓ Spouse or domestic partner
  • ✓ Children (including step-children)
  • ✓ Parents
  • ✓ Siblings
  • ✓ Grandparents

Important Guidelines

  • Make calls in person whenever possible
  • Have another family member present for support
  • Be clear but compassionate in delivering the news
  • Have important details ready (hospital, funeral home, etc.)

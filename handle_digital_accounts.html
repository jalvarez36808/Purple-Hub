Step-by-Step Process

1. Inventory Accounts
  • Check email for account statements
  • Review browser bookmarks
  • Check password managers
  • Look through financial records
2. Gather Documentation
  • Death certificate
  • Proof of authority (executor documents)
  • Government ID
  • Account information
3. Contact Platforms
  • Follow platform-specific procedures
  • Submit required documentation
  • Request account closure or memorialization
  • Download important data

Major Platform Procedures

Choose between:

  • Memorialization (preserves account)
  • Deletion (removes account)

Security Best Practices

  • Never share login credentials
  • Use secure methods to store account information
  • Keep detailed records of all actions taken
  • Consider using a password manager

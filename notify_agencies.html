Notifying Government Agencies

Time Sensitive

Most agencies should be notified within 30 days of death to prevent benefit overpayment or identity theft.

Priority Notifications

Agency Deadline Action
Social Security Administration Immediately Contact SSA
IRS Next tax season Contact IRS
Veterans Affairs (if applicable) 30 days Contact VA

Required Documents

Essential Documents
  • Death certificate (multiple copies)
  • Social Security numbers
  • Birth certificate
  • Marriage certificate (if applicable)
  • Military discharge papers (if applicable)
Additional Documents
  • Most recent tax return
  • Insurance policies
  • Bank account information
  • Outstanding bills
  • Property deeds

Helpful Tips

  • Keep detailed records of all communications
  • Request multiple copies of death certificates
  • Follow up if you don't receive responses
  • Consider using certified mail for important documents

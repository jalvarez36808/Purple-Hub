Ordering a Death Certificate

There are several ways to obtain a death certificate:

  • Online: Use websites like VitalChek for fast access.
  • By Mail: Submit a completed form, identification, and fee to the local vital records office.
  • In Person: Visit the vital records office or county clerk’s office where the death occurred.
  • Through a Funeral Home or Attorney: They can request the certificate on your behalf.

Important Information:

  • Know the state and county where the death occurred.
  • Identification is required to prove relation to the deceased (exceptions: funeral directors, attorneys).
  • Fees range from $10-$25. Expedited options are available for mail-in requests.

# Purple Hub

Purple Hub is a compassionate, web-based platform designed to assist individuals who have lost a loved one, offering a structured, step-by-step checklist to guide them through this difficult time. Our goal is to provide an accessible and supportive experience, ensuring users have all the necessary tools and resources in one place.

## Our Mission

At Purple Hub, our mission is to provide a clear, actionable path for those managing the overwhelming process that follows the passing of a loved one. We aim to deliver practical guidance, emotional support, and structured resources to help individuals and families navigate this challenging period.

## Key Features

- **Interactive Step-by-Step Checklist**: A structured, easy-to-follow guide covering everything from obtaining a death certificate to financial and emotional support.
- **Resource Database**: Direct access to relevant financial assistance programs, government aid, and bereavement support.
- **Emotional Support Section**: Articles, support group links, and coping strategies tailored to grieving individuals.
- **Task Progress Tracker**: Users can track their progress, mark tasks as completed, and get reminders for important steps.
- **Nationwide & Local Assistance**: Resources for contacting appropriate agencies, support networks, and state-specific services.

## How It Works

- **Step-by-Step Navigation**: Follow our interactive checklist, which categorizes tasks based on time frames (immediately, within days, after two weeks).
- **Quick-Access Popups & Full Guides**: Key tasks are summarized in popups, with "Read More" links leading to detailed guides when necessary.
- **Progress Tracking**: Mark items as complete, automatically updating your checklist status.
- **Assistance on Demand**: Find financial, emotional, and community support options tailored to your needs.

## Future Enhancements (Version 2)
